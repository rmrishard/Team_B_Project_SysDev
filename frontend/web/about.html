About Yalla Habibi

Our Story

Bringing the authentic taste of Lebanon to Canada.

Yalla Habibi Imports was founded with a simple mission: to share the rich culinary heritage of Lebanon with our Canadian community. We carefully select each product, working directly with Lebanese producers to ensure the highest quality and authenticity.

Our name "Yalla Habibi" reflects our warm, welcoming approach to sharing Lebanese culture through its finest products.

Our Promise

  • Authenticity: Direct from Lebanon's finest producers
  • Quality: Rigorous selection process for every product
  • Freshness: Regular shipments to ensure product quality
  • Service: Dedicated support for all your needs
